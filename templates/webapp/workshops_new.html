About


What is Cisco networking? Ever wondered how the internet works? Ever got the thought to learn how your router works and all the protocols and the methods used? Are you bored of studying about old age communication techniques like AM and FM? Elan & ηvision, IIT Hyderabad in association with Azure Skynet presents to you this workshop on Cisco Networking. This workshop introduces you to the knowledge of present world’s networking devices and techniques. Participants will be introduced to Networking devices, Addressing, Routing, different protocols etc.


Date : 9th and 10th February
Venue : IIT Hyderabad
Timing : Friday 1:00 PM to 8:30 PM
Saturday 10:00 Am to 6:00 PM
Fee - ₹ 1000/- per student
*No prerequisites required
