A Workshop by Abhinaya Murthy

10th Feb, 2018

11am - 1pm

Abhinaya Murthy

Abhinaya Murthy has been performing poetry at open mics and several platforms across the country for 2 years. A finalist at the slam poetry contest in Delhi at India's first Mental Health Festival, she also got her anthology of poems published in 'Out of the Cuckoon' at the age of 15. 

Abhinaya has won several slam poetry competitions, including the prestigious one at the Annapurna International School of Film and Media, Hyderabad and was the finalist at the Toast Master Internationals Master Orator Championship in 2015 at ISB Hyderabad. 

What she will look to cover in the workshop is giving beginners and performers practical tips for performing, writing and experiencing poetry.
