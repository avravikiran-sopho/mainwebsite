About


What is Cisco networking? Ever wondered how the internet works? Ever got the thought to learn how your router works and all the protocols and the methods used? Are you bored of studying about old age communication techniques like AM and FM? Elan & ηvision, IIT Hyderabad in association with Azure Skynet presents to you this workshop on Cisco Networking.
