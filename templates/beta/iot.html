IoT-Home Automation Workshop

About


How beautiful it is when your door can update it's Facebook Status every time someone opens it. A song starts playing when you tweet about it. A fire sensor automatically sends an E-Mail to the fire department. Keeping all the awesomeness in mind we have introduced the Internet of things workshop to get you ready for the future.

"The Internet of Things isn't a concept. It's a network; true technology based network of all networks."
-Edewede Oriwoh

Elan & ηvision, IIT Hyderabad in association with TechieNest presents to you a workshop on Internet of Things applied in home automation. Attend the workshop and learn how to control home appliances from anywhere in the world wirelessly using Wi-Fi technology.In this workshop you'll learn the basics of embedded systems, digital electronics and you'll be introduced to the Arduino platform, Wi-Fi module and many more.


Venue : IIT Hyderabad
Date : 26th and 27th August
Timing : 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM
Fee per person - ₹ 1500/-

Registrations Closed !!

Highlights


  • Gain keen knowledge on fundamentals of embedded systems
  • A hands-on session where one can build & develop your own Wi-Fi controlled wireless home appliances system
  • Gain conceptual knowledge on Embedded System, Embedded C++, Arduino, Relay, Wi-Fi etc.
  • Workshop taken by exceptionally qualified and professional trainers actively involved in research & development.
  • Certificate from Elan & ηvision, IIT Hyderabad
  • Study material (soft copy) and relevant software to each student
  • Live Demos and Interactive Question & Answer sessions
  • Kit for a team of 5

Outline


Day 1- Session 1:
  • Understanding of IoT Market perspective
  • Future Aspects of IoT
  • Real World IoT design Constrains, Industrial automation and commercial building Automation in IoT
  • Introduction to controlling devices
  • Wide description about controlling devices
  • Input & Output peripherals in controlling devices
  • Resisters in controlling devices
  • Programming of controlling devices
Day 1- Session 2:
  • Introduction to relay
  • Types of relay and its working
  • Introduction to ULN2803 IC
Day 2- Session 1:
  • Interfacing between Arduino and ULN2803
  • UART in Arduino
  • Accessing internal UART
  • Transferring data serially from PC to Arduino
  • Introduction and interfacing of Wireless technology
Day 2- Session 2:
  • Introduction to Internet
  • Introduction to TCIP
  • Introduction to IP addressing and its different classes
  • IP address distribution to PAN India
  • Generating access point using Wi Fi technology
  • Word wide accessing and controlling of secure automation system
  • Q&A Session


Practicals
  • Different patterns of LED
  • Development of an Automation System using Arduino Development Board
  • Smart Automation using PC
  • Local network control office automation
  • Generating different class of IP
  • Generating different IPs to client
  • Generating access point using Wi Fi technology
  • World-wide accessing and controlling of secure automation system
